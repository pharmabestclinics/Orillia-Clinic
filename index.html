About Us

Caring for Orillia,
One Patient at a Time

Orillia Downtown Walk-In Clinic is part of the PharmaBest Group, a trusted network of healthcare providers across Ontario. Located in the heart of downtown Orillia, we're committed to making quality healthcare accessible to everyone in our community.

Our clinic offers a welcoming, judgement-free environment where every patient is treated with dignity and respect. Whether you're new to Orillia or have lived here your whole life, we're here for you.
